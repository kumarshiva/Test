About igmri

The IGMRI, located at Hapur functions under the supervision and administrative control of the Storage and Research Divisionx of the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution, Department of Food and Public Distribution.

Vision

Creation of skilled manpower on scientific storage of foodgrains to preserve its quality and minimize losses.

Safe and scientific storage of foodgrains procured by the Government.

objectives

Institutional training on quality control, pest control, scientific storage practices, preservation and inspection of foodgrains, scientific storage of foodgrains. Create awareness about safe storage of foodgrains
